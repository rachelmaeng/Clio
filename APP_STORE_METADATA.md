## Description (4000 characters max)

Clio helps you understand your body by tracking your wellness in harmony with your menstrual cycle.

**FEEL YOUR BEST EVERY DAY**

Your body changes throughout your cycle—and so should your approach to wellness. Clio provides personalized insights for eating, movement, and self-care based on where you are in your menstrual cycle.

**FEATURES**

• Cycle Phase Tracking
Know exactly which phase you're in—menstrual, follicular, ovulation, or luteal—and get tailored recommendations for each.

• Daily Check-Ins
Quick, thoughtful check-ins help you track how your body feels. Log body sensations, mood, and notes to discover patterns over time.

• Smart Meal Logging
Track your meals and see how different foods affect how you feel. Get phase-appropriate food suggestions to support your body's needs.

• Movement Tracking
Log workouts and sync with Apple Health. Discover which types of movement work best for each phase of your cycle.

• Personal Insights
Clio learns from your data to surface meaningful patterns—like which foods energize you during ovulation or what helps you feel better during your period.

**PRIVACY FIRST**

Your health data stays on your device. We don't have access to your personal information, and we never sell your data.

**DESIGNED WITH CARE**

Clio features a beautiful, calming design that makes tracking feel like self-care, not a chore. Gentle illustrations and a warm color palette create a peaceful experience.

**APPLE HEALTH INTEGRATION**

Optionally sync your workouts and activity data with Apple Health to keep all your wellness information in one place.

Start your journey to cycle-aware wellness today. Download Clio and feel more connected to your body.
