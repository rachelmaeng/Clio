# Privacy Policy for Clio

**Last Updated: January 28, 2025**

## Overview

Clio ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal health and wellness tracking app designed to help you understand your body in sync with your menstrual cycle. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name and email address
- **Menstrual Cycle Data**: Last period start date, cycle length, and period length
- **Body Metrics**: Height, weight, birth year, and activity level (optional)
- **Health Goals**: Calorie goals and fitness objectives (optional)
- **Fertility Goals**: Conception-related preferences (optional)
- **Dietary Information**: Food allergies and meal logs
- **Wellness Data**: Daily check-ins, mood, body sensations, and notes
- **Movement Data**: Workout logs and activity information

### Information from Apple Health (Optional)
With your explicit permission, Clio may read and write the following data to Apple Health:
- Workout data
- Active energy burned
- Exercise minutes

## How Your Data is Stored

**All your personal data is stored locally on your device.** Clio does not transmit your health information to external servers. Your data remains private and under your control.

- Data is stored using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- Your information never leaves your device
- We do not have access to your personal health data

## How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:
- Provide personalized cycle-phase insights and recommendations
- Track your meals, movement, and wellness patterns
- Generate personal insights based on your logged data
- Sync with Apple Health (only if you enable this feature)

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.**

Your data may only be shared:
- With Apple Health, if you explicitly grant permission
- As required by law or legal process

## Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your logged data within the app
- **Delete**: Remove individual entries or all data at any time
- **Apple Health**: Revoke Health access in your device Settings
- **Export**: Your data remains on your device and can be managed through iOS

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- All data stored locally using Apple's secure frameworks
- No cloud storage or external transmission of health data
- App follows Apple's data protection guidelines

## Children's Privacy

Clio is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
